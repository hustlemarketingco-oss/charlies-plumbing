Frequently Asked Questions
Do you offer same-day service? +
Yes — we offer same-day service 7 days a week, including weekends and holidays. For emergency situations like burst pipes or sewage backups, we have technicians on call 24 hours a day. Call us at (801) 406-8585 and we'll get someone out as fast as possible.
How does your pricing work? +
We believe in upfront, transparent pricing. Before any work begins, your technician will assess the situation and provide a clear, flat-rate quote. You approve the price before we start — no surprises on the final bill, ever.
Are your plumbers licensed and insured? +
Absolutely. All of our technicians hold valid Utah plumbing licenses, are fully insured and bonded, and have passed background checks. You can trust who you're letting into your home.
What areas do you serve? +
We serve the entire Wasatch Front including Salt Lake City, West Valley, Provo, Orem, Sandy, Murray, Draper, West Jordan, South Jordan, Lehi, American Fork, Taylorsville, Midvale, and surrounding areas. Not sure if you're in our service area? Just call us and we'll let you know.
Can I book online or after hours? +
Yes! Our AI receptionist answers calls 24/7, even after business hours. You can call (801) 406-8585 any time of day or night and our system will gather your information and schedule your appointment. You'll receive a confirmation text immediately.
Do you offer a warranty on your work? +
Yes. All of our labor is backed by a satisfaction guarantee. If something isn't right after we've completed a job, call us and we'll come back and make it right at no charge. We stand behind every service call.
How do I know if I need drain cleaning vs sewer repair? +
If you have one slow drain, it's likely a local clog that drain cleaning can fix. If multiple drains are slow or backing up, or if you notice gurgling sounds, sewage smells, or wet spots in your yard, you may have a main sewer line issue. Our technicians can run a camera inspection to diagnose the problem accurately.
What should I do in a plumbing emergency? +
First, locate your main water shut-off valve and turn off the water supply to stop flooding. Then call us immediately at (801) 406-8585 — we have emergency technicians available 24/7. Don't attempt major repairs yourself as this can cause additional damage.
